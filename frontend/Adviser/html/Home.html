Follow these steps to properly document and submit a student referral to the Guidance Office. Each step is essential to ensure the student receives appropriate support and intervention.
1

visibility Observation

Monitor and identify student behaviors, academic performance, or concerns that may require guidance intervention. Pay attention to changes in behavior, attendance patterns, academic struggles, or social-emotional issues.

ðŸ’¡ Tip: Document specific dates, times, and contexts when observing concerning behaviors
2

description Documentation

Record detailed notes about the observed situation, including specific incidents, dates, frequency, and any previous interventions attempted. Gather relevant academic records or reports if applicable.

ðŸ’¡ Tip: Be objective and factual in your documentation, avoiding subjective judgments
3

edit_document Complete Referral Form

Fill out the official student referral form with complete information including student details, nature of concern, severity level, category (academic, behavioral, social-emotional), and your recommendations.

ðŸ’¡ Tip: Navigate to "My Students" page to access and complete the referral form
4

send Submit for Review

Submit the completed referral form to the Guidance Office. The referral will be marked as "Pending" and queued for review by guidance counselors. You will be notified of status changes.

ðŸ’¡ Tip: Check this dashboard regularly to track the status of your submitted referrals
5

psychology Guidance Counselor Intervention

The guidance counselor will review the referral, assess the situation, and provide appropriate intervention. Status will update to "For Consultation" or "In Progress" as the case is handled. You may be contacted for additional information or follow-up.

ðŸ’¡ Tip: Remain available to collaborate with the guidance counselor throughout the process
